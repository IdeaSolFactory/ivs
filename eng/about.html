World-class
team and equipmnet

Founded in 1990, INTERTON Group designs, implements and maintains audio, lighting, staging, visual and control systems, and distributes AV products from leading manufacturers. INTERTON Group develops and manufactures its own audiovisual equipment under the IVS brand name, and creates and manages digital content ecosystems. “Our professional, business achievements performed over the past decades hallmarks our present and our future.

Our obligation to fulfill the needs of our partners requires the highest level of self-commitment. We are proud that INTERTON Group is acknowledged among the market leaders in the AV industry.” /Géza Balogh, CEO, Owner/

Our history

  • 1967-1989

    At the development of the Budapest Electroacoustic Factory (BEAG), he develops several products with his colleagues Géza Balogh. They gain great professional knowledge and practice.

  • 1970-1989

    The result of developmental research work: patented acoustic equipment, acoustic systems in large numbers. Loudspeakers, microphones, surge suppressors, digital sound systems

  • 1980

    HTP 90 column speakers were installed in Luzsnyik Olimpic Stadion, Moscow

  • 1985-90

    A Cardioid studio monitors were installed in the Hungarian National Radio

  • 1989-1990

    The first PC controlled conference system installation in the Hungarian Parliament

  • 1990

    Géza Balogh defended his dissertation and founded Interton Kft. With some of his acoustic colleagues, including his wife.

  • 1991

    Dr. Géza Balogh PhD develops HOK series cardiod column speaker (first generation)

  • 1992-1993

    First acoustically monitored PA system installation at the Hungarian Nuclear Power Plant with more than 600 loudspeakers

  • 2005

    Dr. Géza Balogh PhD develops the DCR system, the first generation of digitally controlled, non-inline room acoustics enhancement system

  • 2005

    Installation of the DCR system in Kölcsey Congress Centre, Debrecen

  • 2007

    Installation of VSK series line source speaker in the Baroque Court

  • 2008-2011

    Dr. Géza Balogh PhD develops IVS series hypercardiod line source speaker (thrid generation)

  • 2010

    Kodály Centre, Pécs – the launch of third generation of high directivity line source speaker named IVS

  • 2011-2012

    Dr. Géza Balogh PhD develops the AURA system, the second generation of digitally controlled, non-inline room acoustics enhancement system

  • 2014

    Installation of a significant IVS system in the Keleti Railway Station, Budapest

  • 2017-2019

    We developed 2nd order cardioid loudspeakers and planar loudspeakers in the R&D project GINOP-2.1.7-15-2016-01023

  • 2019

    We applied to patent application with the results of our 2nd order cardioid development
