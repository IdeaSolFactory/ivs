Technology
Go sound!

The dispersion capabilities of traditional speakers, column speakers and planar speakers are not efficient enough at lower frequencies, because they tend to worsen acoustic experience and affect speech intelligibility. The cardioid speakers of IVS product family are made to handle this phenomenon at middle and low frequencies below 1000 Hz. .

Two classes
of cardioid speakers

The first-class/b> cardioid speakers beam with full energy to the front, quarter to the sides, and theoritically zero backwards, but tenth at most in real applications.

The second-class cardioid speakers beam with full energy to the front, sixteenth to the sides, and theoritically zero backwards, but sixteenth at most in real applications.
